ABOUT ME

Lea was born and raised in Tangier, Spain in 1930.

In those days' tangier belonged to Spain and when she was twenty, she came to Israel with her family.

In Israel she met my grandfather, Miguel (in Israel his name Meir), and they got married.

They lived in Beer Sheva for some years and then moved to Bat-Yam.

Lea likes to read books in Spanish or French.

Her specialties food are Pastel which is similar to shepherd pie,

meat stew that called Dafina which made with potatoes, eggs, meat and chick peas.
